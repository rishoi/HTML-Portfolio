The best malayalam movies according to Risha

My top 3 movies are

Drishyam (2013) – Directed by Jeethu Joseph

A landmark in Malayalam cinema, Drishyam is a gripping family thriller centered around Georgekutty, an ordinary man who goes to extraordinary lengths to protect his family after an unexpected crime. The screenplay is tight, emotionally powerful, and full of suspense. It changed the face of Indian thrillers and was remade in multiple languages, proving its universal appeal.

Manichitrathazhu (1993) – Directed by Fazil

A classic psychological thriller blended with horror, Manichitrathazhu is known for its brilliant storytelling and powerful performances, especially by Shobana. The movie explores themes of mental illness and superstition with elegance. Its balance of tradition, psychology, and entertainment makes it a timeless favorite in Indian cinema.

Kireedam (1989) – Directed by Sibi Malayil

A heart-wrenching drama that tells the story of Sethumadhavan, a young man whose dream of becoming a police officer is shattered due to fate and societal pressures. With a stellar performance by Mohanlal and a realistic narrative, Kireedam deeply explores the tragic transformation of a common man into a victim of circumstances. The emotional weight and social commentary make it unforgettable.
